Cybersecurity

Information

Cybersecurity refers to the practices, technologies, and processes designed to protect digital information, computer systems, and networks from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction. With the rise of technology, cyber threats have become more sophisticated, including malware, phishing, ransomware, and denial of service attacks. To stay safe, individuals and organizations can follow best practices such as using strong passwords, keeping software up-to-date, being cautious with emails, and using encryption. Cybersecurity careers are also in high demand, with roles like security analysts, penetration testers, and incident responders. Additionally, resources like NIST, CISA, and SANS Institute provide guidance and support for cybersecurity efforts. By understanding and addressing cybersecurity risks, we can protect our digital assets and maintain trust in the technology that powers our lives.
